Following the AES 2023 meeting in Orlando, FL, we were strongly motivated to see the neuroimaging community in epilepsy grow and reach new heights of collaboration and leadership.

This inspiration fuelled the creation of NEW: Neuroimaging of Epilepsy Worldwide; a community-powered platform for trainees, researchers, and clinicians interested in learning, sharing, and building their careers in neuroimaging of epilepsy.

Our vision for this initiative consists of keeping the community informed of current opportunities in epilepsy research (conferences, job ads, funding) and providing a strong community composed of ECRs and established researchers to facilitate networking, collaboration, and mentorship amongst members.
